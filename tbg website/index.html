About TBG

Local Roots.
Global Standards.

Founded in 2008 by two brothers amid a war-torn Iraq, TBG is a family-owned service company built on deep local knowledge and proven delivery for international clients.

TBG enjoys excellent knowledge of Iraq's local customs, logistics needs, and business environment — maintaining strong links with well-placed corporate entities for effective, timely service delivery.

Mission
Best-in-class essential service support with professional dedication, enabling clients to focus on their core objectives.
Vision
A leading service provider in a prosperous Iraq — investing in local employment and career development for Iraqi youth and women.
Values
Respect, loyalty, responsibility, generosity, and hospitality — traditional Iraqi values at the core of every client engagement.
